## Summary

The GABAG Operations Platform is a custom AI-powered operations platform for a Swiss manufacturer of sanitary installation systems. The platform will automate the order-to-delivery flow and lead outreach pipeline, with Mentor Q remaining the system of record for accounting throughout the transition. The project will be delivered in 10 weeks, with five logical milestones and a staged rollout to minimize operational risk.
